Challange 1

A salesperson who walks door to door to sell goods carries a suitcase with him.
Each morning he packs his suitcase with items from his inventory.
Each item has a size (cm3), price (Rs) and weight (grams).
Write a javascript programme to calculate the total price of the items he can carry in his suitcase without exceeding the max weight and size limits of the suitcase.

Products list:
Item 01: weight: 10, price: 20, size: 30
Item 02: weight: 15, price: 25, size: 35
Item 03: weight: 20, price: 30, size: 40
Item 04: weight: 25, price: 35, size: 40
